Sauteed Brussel Sprouts

Brussels sprouts are a delicious and healthy addition to any weeknight meal! They are rich in Vitamin A and C and contain antioxidants which can help improve the texture of your skin and add years to your life. So I often reminisce on my childhood when I sauteed Brussel sprouts for my husband and kids. It is bittersweet for me but it is quite a dashing side-dish. You must simply get this recipe!!

Ingredients
  • 1lbs brussel sprouts
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  1. Using a cutting board, cut the brussel sprouts in half, making sure to cut off the root at the bottom of the vegetable
  2. In a mid-size pan, over medium heat, melt the 2 tbsp of coconut oil
  3. Once the oil has warmed up, add the brussel sprouts to the pan
  4. Stir the brussel sprouts, making sure to coat all of them with oil
  5. Add the salt and pepper making sure to distribute them evenly
  6. Cover pan, making sure to leave the cover slightly ajar so steam does not build inside the pan
  7. After 2 minutes, take off lid and cook the brussel sprouts for 7 minutes making sure to stir them ocasionally
  8. Enjoy!
