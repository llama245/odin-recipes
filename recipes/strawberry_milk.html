Ingredients
  • 1 liter coconut milk
  • 1 lbs fresh strawberries (do not use frozen or freeze-dried!)
  • Large pitcher
Steps
  1. Wash strawberries throughly and cut them into 1 centimeter pieces
  2. Add them to pitcher
  3. Add milk
  4. Add sugar to taste if desired
  5. Leave pticher in the refrigerator for a minimum of 8 hours
  6. Stir & Enjoy!
